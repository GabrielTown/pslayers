Intro.

Welcome to the only Photoshop tutorial (focusing on layers) you'll need today and possibly tomorrow. After completion you'll be a wizard at three Photoshop layer basics:

  • Visibility
  • Opacity
  • Blend Options

Let's get started. To the right or below you'll see an image and a Layers sidebar.

Visibility.

If we look to the left of each layer on the sidebar we can see an eye. Go ahead and click the eyes on the sidebar, adjacent to the woman's image, to toggle the visibility of the three layers. This should give us a good idea of the position (top, middle, bottom) of the three layers. Also, we don't always want to see every layer when editing in Photoshop. Visibility allows us to focus on one or two layers at a time. Hint: when we see a checkered pattern this means that part of our picture is invisible. If we save the image as a PNG file the checkered pattern takes on the color of whatever background the image is on.
